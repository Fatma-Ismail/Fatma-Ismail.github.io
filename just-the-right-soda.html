Just the Right Soda

This package design was created for a fictional beverage company presented with a hip, modern voice. I wanted to present this soda as a healthy alternative to more mainstream carbonated beverages.

Concept
Refreshing new soda!

Challenges
Distilling the identifying characteristics of each fruit into a geometric icon that remained easily recognizable.

Visual Strategy
I was inspired by the Swiss Typographic Style of design and I wanted to design a unique label that would stand out in any supermarket shelf amongst the busy labels traditionally found on soda bottles.

Connotation
Contemporary, young and Swiss
