Food For thought

Research, Prototyping, UX/UI Design | 2015

Food for thought is an application that provides access to free food for students with limited resources.
It's a collaborative project, as a part of the user experience course.

Challenges

Focusing on the target audience by conducting user testing at every stage to ensure functionality. Prioritizing the content and technical features for the initial app release.

Design Approach

Started with user interviews of food bank representatives and students to understand their needs. Followed by competitive analysis, persona and a sitemap. Usability was a key concern. 

Role
In Team of three, my role was UX/UI, logo design, wireframes and document design.
