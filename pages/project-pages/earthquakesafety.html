Earthquake Safety Card

Concept, Icon Design and Layout | 2015

This informational card was designed for English-speaking people in Egypt. The goal was to make earthquake safety instructions
easy to remember and visually attractive and culturally relevant.

Concept
Reading earthquake safety card could be fun.

Challenges
Linking the content and form in a representational way.

Visual Strategy
I chose to show the earthquake safety card in a whimsical fashion that is both acceptable and engaging for a broad audience. The slab serif typeface and the yellow colors were key into common perceptions of Egypt.

Connotation
Whimsical, Modern and Entertaining.
