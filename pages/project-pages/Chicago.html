Chicago

I designed and produced 11x17in poster, for Musical Play “Chicago” for the CCSF’s Theatre and Music Department targeting CCSF students, non-students, friends, family, and anyone who is fond of theatre and musicals.

Challenges
I had to make sure that the message is clear with the
restriction of a 2-spot-color offset printing process.

Illustrator
Indesign
