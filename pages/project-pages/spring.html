Spring Concert

The goal of this project was to design a poster for the CCSF music department’s 2016 spring concert. 

Concept
The beauty of the spring

Challenges
Adjusting the colors of the flowers in Photoshop and adding the watercolor effect.

Visual Strategy
I wanted to show the poster as an elegant invitation to the spring concert, using the watercolors to convey a handcrafted feel appropriate to the event’s maturity.

Connotation
Spring, Inviting and Dreamy
