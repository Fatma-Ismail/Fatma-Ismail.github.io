Alola

Alola is a smart application for personal pantry management, and shopping for healthy food. The application is being built by a startup located in San Francisco. It learns about user's habits and creates a personalized grocery list for them and their household each week. I designed the app UX/UI and the brand identity of the startup.

Challenges
Simplifying the signing up process
and understanding what triggers different personas to buy grocery online. Finishing the whole brand identity in less than a week 

Design Approach
Understanding the business needs and prospective client needs from the platform. Prioritized key functionality needed for initial app release. Collaborated with the CEO to maintain cohesive design.
